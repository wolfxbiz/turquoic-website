## Quick Answer: Website Cost in Trivandrum

| Website Type | Typical Price Range |
|---|---|
| Basic business website (5 pages) | ₹15,000 – ₹35,000 |
| Professional business website | ₹35,000 – ₹80,000 |
| Ecommerce store (Shopify/WooCommerce) | ₹60,000 – ₹1,50,000 |
| Custom web application | ₹1,50,000 – ₹5,00,000+ |
| Landing page (single page) | ₹8,000 – ₹25,000 |

Prices vary based on design complexity, number of pages, features, and whether you need ongoing SEO and maintenance.

## What's Included in These Prices?

A well-priced website from a Trivandrum agency should include:

- **Custom design** — not a generic template
- **Mobile-responsive layout** — critical since 70%+ of Kerala users browse on mobile
- **Basic on-page SEO** — proper meta tags, heading structure, image alt text
- **Contact form and WhatsApp integration**
- **Google Analytics setup**
- **1 month of post-launch support**

If an agency quotes ₹5,000 for a "full website," they're giving you a template with no SEO and no support. You'll pay more to fix it later.

## What Drives the Cost Up?

### 1. Number of Pages
A 5-page brochure site costs far less than a 30-page service website with individual landing pages for each service.

### 2. Custom Functionality
Booking systems, member portals, calculators, custom dashboards — each adds development time and cost.

### 3. Ecommerce Features
Payment gateway integration (Razorpay, PayU), inventory management, order tracking, and multi-variant products all add to the scope.

### 4. SEO Depth
Basic SEO is included in most packages. But a full SEO strategy — keyword research, content planning, schema markup, backlink building — is a separate investment.

### 5. Ongoing Maintenance
Most agencies in Trivandrum charge ₹3,000–₹10,000/month for hosting, updates, and security monitoring.

## Why Cheap Websites Are Expensive in the Long Run

Many businesses in Trivandrum get burned by cheap ₹5,000–₹10,000 websites that:

- Load slowly (3–5 seconds) and get penalized by Google
- Are not mobile-optimized
- Have no SSL certificate
- Can't be updated without calling the developer
- Have no analytics tracking so you don't know if it's working

A properly built website is an asset. A cheap one is a liability.

## How to Compare Quotes from Trivandrum Web Agencies

When you get quotes, ask these questions:

1. Is the design custom or a template?
2. Is it mobile-first?
3. What is the page speed score you're targeting?
4. Is on-page SEO included?
5. What happens after launch — do you provide support?
6. Who owns the domain and hosting after the project?

If an agency can't answer these confidently, move on.
